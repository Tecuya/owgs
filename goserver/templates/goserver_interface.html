What is OWGS?

OWGS is a free web application enabling go/baduk/wei-qi players to find one another, play games online, review games and chat.

OWGS runs in your browser in pure javascript; no plugins are required. OWGS is an event-driven real-time server; your moves are instantly visible to the other player(s) and vice-versa, without polling. OWGS is an open source application, free to use and modify.
